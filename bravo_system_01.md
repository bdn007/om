## 1.         SYSTEM OVERVIEW

### 1.1. PURPOSE

The Fire and Gas System (FGS) includes Detection and Logic systems, monitors the Fire and Gas Detectors, and initiates appropriate actions.
The first aim of the FGS is to mitigate the effects of a fire and/or gas releases to protect the personnel, the environment, and the equipment.
The FGS:

-         Provides, via the PA/GA system, a fast, audible, and visual indication of a fire and/or gas release.  
-         Indicates on ICSS HMI the areas where a fire and/or gas release is detected.  
-         Triggers executive actions: ESD activation, deluge activation, closing of the fire dampers, etc.  
### 1.2. SYSTEM CRITICALITY

As it preserves the safety of the personnel and the integrity of the equipment, the Fire and Gas system is one of the most critical systems of the B-Location. In the event that the FGS is not available, the B-Location shall not be operated and/or shall be shutdown. In case of unavailability of the FGS only in a particular fire zone, B-Location may operate in a downgrade situation.
